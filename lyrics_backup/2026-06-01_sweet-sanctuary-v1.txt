[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Close the door and leave the world outside,
Come a little closer, let the shadows hide.
I’ve been waiting for this moment all day,
To pull you in and let the quiet stay.

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers through the pouring rain,
I hear the echo of a distant train.
But all I care about is breathing you in,
Tracing the poetry across your skin.

[Pre-Chorus]
The fire’s burning down to ember glow,
There’s nowhere else that we need to go.
Your eyes are telling me a secret truth,
And I’m getting lost in the taste of you.

[Chorus]
You are my sweet sanctuary tonight,
Wrapped in the warmth of the fading light.
Every whisper, every touch we share,
Is a silent promise floating in the air.

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, sweet sanctuary, hold me close,
(Hold me close, don't let me go)

[Verse 2]
A glass of bourbon and a worn-out chair,
The scent of midnight tangled in your hair.
We don’t need words to say how we feel,
This kind of magic is entirely real.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The fire’s burning down to ember glow,
There’s nowhere else that we need to go.
Your eyes are telling me a secret truth,
And I’m getting lost in the taste of you.

[Chorus]
You are my sweet sanctuary tonight,
Wrapped in the warmth of the fading light.
Every whisper, every touch we share,
Is a silent promise floating in the air.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, sweet sanctuary, hold me close,
(Hold me close, don't let me go)

[Bridge]
I never knew a love could run this deep,
A quiet promise that I want to keep.
You fall into my arms, the perfect space,
While moonlight leaves a shadow on your face.
I’m holding onto every breath we share,
Just know that Saeed will always meet you there.

[Spoken]
I want to freeze this moment.
Just you, me, and the midnight air.

[Final Chorus]
You are my sweet sanctuary tonight,
Wrapped in the warmth of the fading light.
Every whisper, every touch we share,
Is a silent promise floating in the air.
